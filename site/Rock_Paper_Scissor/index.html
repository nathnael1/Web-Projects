  • Rock crushes Scissors: If a player chooses Rock and the other chooses Scissors, Rock wins.
  • Scissors cuts Paper: If a player chooses Scissors and the other chooses Paper, Scissors wins.
  • Paper covers Rock: If a player chooses Paper and the other chooses Rock, Paper wins.
  • Tie/Draw: If both players choose the same option, the game is a tie or draw.

The player who wins a round is determined by the rules above, and the game typically consists of a series of rounds.
